Ask FYEO is a web-based resource for first year students. This tool is available to you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and 365 days a year.

Search Tips:

  • Ask your question in the form of a complete sentence like "How do I enroll in classes? How do I swap around sections?"
  • Ask simple questions.
  • Ask just one question at a time.
